What is WenXen?

WenXen is a privacy-first, client-side web application that helps you manage your XEN Crypto ecosystem assets. Available at wenxen.com or as a download, everything runs directly in your browser - no servers, no tracking, no data collection. Whether you use our hosted version or run it locally, your data never leaves your device.

Track Everything

Monitor all your XEN mints, stakes, XENFTs, and vXEN positions across Ethereum and Base networks in one unified view.

100% Private

All data stays in your browser's storage. Even when using wenxen.com, we can't see your data - it never leaves your device.

Lightning Fast

Smart caching and efficient scanning algorithms mean you get real-time updates without the wait.

Multi-Chain

Seamlessly switch between Ethereum mainnet and Base L2, with automatic network detection and configuration.

Advanced filtering, sorting, and export options help you understand your portfolio at a glance.

Beautiful UI

Clean, modern interface with light and dark themes. Designed for both beginners and power users.

Quick Start Guide

  1. Connect Your Wallet - Click "Connect Wallet" and choose MetaMask or Rabby
  2. Configure Settings - Add your Etherscan/BaseScan API keys in the Settings tab (optional but recommended)
  3. Scan Your Holdings - Hit the scan buttons to discover all your XEN ecosystem assets
  4. Explore Your Data - Use filters, search, and sort to analyze your portfolio

Pro Tip: The scanner remembers your data between sessions, so you only need to scan for new transactions!

Why WenXen?

Built by the community, for the community. WenXen was created to solve a real problem: tracking XEN assets across multiple chains and contracts is complex. We believe in the XEN principles of self-custody, transparency, and decentralization - that's why WenXen runs entirely in your browser.

Use it at wenxen.com or download it. Either way, no middlemen. No servers. No nonsense. Just you and your data. We're merely hosting static files - the app runs 100% on your machine.
