What is WenXen.com?

WenXen is a privacy-first, client-side web application that helps you manage your XEN Crypto ecosystem assets. Available at wenxen.com or as a download, the tracker runs directly in your browser. Portfolio data and keys stay local; on wenxen.com, usage analytics are enabled by default and can be disabled in Settings.

Track, manage & analyze your XEN Crypto holdings across 7 blockchains, from browser with zero dependencies.

🔍 Track Everything

Monitor all your XEN mints, stakes, XENFTs, and vXEN positions across 7 networks (Ethereum, Base, Avalanche, BSC, Moonbeam, Polygon, Optimism) in one unified view.

🔒 100% Private

Portfolio data, addresses, and keys stay in your browser's storage. Even when using wenxen.com, we can't see your tracked holdings.

⚡ Lightning Fast

Smart caching and efficient scanning algorithms mean you get real-time updates without the wait.

🌐 Multi-Chain

Seamlessly switch between 7 blockchain networks with automatic network detection and configuration.

📊 Rich Analytics

Advanced filtering, sorting, and export options help you understand your portfolio at a glance.

🎨 Beautiful UI

Clean, modern interface with light and dark themes. Designed for both beginners and power users.

Quick Start Guide

  1. Add Your Address - Go to Settings and add your wallet addresses
  2. Add API Key - Add your free Etherscan Multichain API key (works for all 7 chains)
  3. Scan Your Holdings - Hit the Scan button to discover all your XEN ecosystem assets
  4. Explore Your Data - Use filters, search, and sort to analyze your portfolio

Pro Tip: The scanner remembers your data between sessions, so you only need to scan for new transactions!

Built by the community, for the community. WenXen was created to solve a real problem: tracking XEN assets across multiple chains and contracts is complex. We believe in the XEN principles of self-custody, transparency, and decentralization - that's why WenXen runs entirely in your browser.

Use it at wenxen.com or download it. Either way, no account, hosted portfolio database, or custody layer. Just you and your data. We're hosting static files - the app runs on your machine.
